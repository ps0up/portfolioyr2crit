Monogram Design

First I was given the task of creating my own personal brand, this kicked off by designing my own Monogram, intially I started drawing up 50 rough ideas for my monogram.



dedede

Moving on from this, I began to narrow down my 50 monogram designs to begin to select my favourite and choose which would be best suited for my own personal brand.


.


Furthering details

I began to draw out some of my monogram designs larger and clearer, adding colour and sketching them to see how they feel in large.




Digital Mockups

I then began to work on my digital versions of my monogram, these were through trial and error and seeing which one worked the best. Neither of these examples, i used however I feel its good to showface my process as the 2nd monogram essentially became changed to become my final version.


The problem I encounered following this is that I didn't know were I wanted my personal branding to take me, some of my designs ended up more serious and formal, looking towards a more webdesign/ux design approach. However, I still wanted to have an illustrative side and have the monogram a bit more lighthearted, relating to my personality.




Final Design
